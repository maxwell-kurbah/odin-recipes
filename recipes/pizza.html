Description

If you're looking for a homemade pizza crust recipe that's great for beginners, you're in luck. This top-rated recipe is super easy to throw together on a whim

Ingredients

  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • Bread flour
  • Olive oil
  • Salt
  1. Gather all ingredients and preheat oven
  2. Place warm water in a bowl and add yeast and sugar
  3. Add flour,oil and salt to the yeast mixture
  4. Let rest for 5 minutes
  5. Turn dough into a pizza base circle
  6. Add sauce and toppings and bake
