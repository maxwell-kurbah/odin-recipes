Description

Wheat dosa. Serve with coconut chutney, sambar, or some other Indian curry.

Ingredients

  • Wheat flour
  • Vegetable oil
  • Pinch salt
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  1. Combine the ingredients in a bowl
  2. Mix till lump free and the batter has good consistency
  3. Heat tablespoon of oil on a griddle over medium heat
  4. Spread the batter in circular motion,slowly yet steadily
  5. Cook until the edges start to brown, 2 to 3 minutes
  6. Repeat with remaining batter,adding more oil in between each batch
